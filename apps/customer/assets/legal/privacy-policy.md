# BarberHero - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BarberHero collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information from customers and barbers using the BarberHero platform.

By creating an account, using BarberHero, booking services, or accepting these policies, you agree to the collection and use of information as described below.

---

## 1. Who We Are

BarberHero is a platform that connects customers with independent freelance barbers.

## 2. Information We Collect

BarberHero may collect and process the following information:

**Personal Information**

- full name
- phone number
- email address
- postcode/address
- profile photos
- barber portfolio images

**Booking Information**

- booking history
- appointment times
- selected services
- pricing information
- cancellation/dispute history

**Payment Information**

Payments are processed securely through third-party payment providers such as Stripe.

BarberHero does not store full card details.

However, we may store:

- payment status
- transaction references
- partial billing information
- refund/dispute information

---

## 3. Location Information

BarberHero may collect approximate or live location information for:

- displaying nearby barbers
- improving booking accuracy
- appointment management
- fraud prevention

Users may disable location permissions through their device settings, although some features may not function correctly.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- create and manage accounts
- process bookings
- process payments
- provide customer support
- improve app performance
- prevent fraud and abuse
- investigate disputes
- comply with legal obligations
- maintain platform security

## 5. Sharing of Information

BarberHero may share limited information when necessary with:

- payment providers
- hosting providers
- cloud storage services
- customer support providers
- legal authorities when legally required

Customer information may be visible to the assigned barber for appointment purposes.

Barber information may be visible to customers through public profiles inside the app.

We do not sell personal information to third parties.
